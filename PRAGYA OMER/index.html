    Citrus limon

It maintains the equilibrium among #vatta, #pitta, & #kapha. #Black circles on face are nowadays a very common problem, ameliorated by it through external application.

    Bidens Pilosa

    #समरा कोकडी, #Black jack, a herbaceous wild medicinal plant of #Asteraceae family known as #Bidens pilosa. It contains a natural functional analog of #retinol i.e., #Bakuchiol, used for #antiaging, #antiwrinkle and #anti-hyperpigmentation of the skin. It shows #antiarthritic, antidiabetic, and anti-inflammatory activities (#phytanic acid). It also exhibits natural #blood thinner activity. It should not be consumed during pregnancy..

    Prunus persica

    Aaru (#आडू) a fruitish plant known as #Prunus persica, a member of almond family-#Rosaceae. It is used therapeutically for curing all types of #age-related disorders including #diabetes, #obesity, #hypertension, #anxiety, #cardiovascular disorders, #neuromuscular problems (#dementia), and #oncological complications. It has high #nutritional value, power house of phenolic and flavonoid antioxidants, macro and micro minerals and enhances the immunity. It is conserved through stem cutting by the treatment of Indole butyric acid (#IBA).

    Fumaria indica

    Indian poppy is a herbaceous plant which is scientifically known as #Fumaria indica (#Indian Fumitory, #पित्तपापड़ा), belonging to #Papaveraceae family.     Mazus pumilus

    #Japanese Mazus-#मालती झार, a herbaceous plant of #Mazaceae family most commonly used as general body tonic. It comprises several antioxidants, vitamins, macro and micro minerals required for enhancing the immunity of the body. It remediates and detoxifies the liver. It’s a good #emmenagogue.     A small perennial herbaceous plant having cordate leaves and pubescent stem belonging to #Violaceae family and commonly known as #Sweet violet/#बनफ्शा.
